DelivAR

An Augmented Reality Tool to expedite Last-Mile Delivery process

Location

Stockholm (Sweden)

Advisor

Prof. Johannes Schöning

Zoe Falomir Llansola

ACK

Airmee AB

Date

September 2018

Category

Augmented Reality

We introduce DelivAR. This app would be a solution for the package identification process, built with an open-source library, called AR.js. We developed a project with the support of a last-mile delivery startup based in Stockholm, Sweden. Technically, this AR application focuses on the recognition of fiducial markers, printed images placed in a real scene that are traced and recognised via video camera.
We evaluated the procedure within a technical study, analysing the technical performances and limitations of the tool. Consequently, we posed a qualitative analysis of the mobile application to measure the subjective consequences of its use. Our approach for this study was a semi-structured interview with an expert in logistics.
